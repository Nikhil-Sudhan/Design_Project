About This Tool

The Optimal College Bus Route Planner helps college administrators plan efficient bus routes for students based on their home locations and bus capacity constraints.

Features:
  • Upload student addresses in CSV format
  • Automatically cluster student locations into optimal bus routes
  • Visualize routes on an interactive map
  • Optimize routes based on bus capacity constraints
  • Generate detailed route reports
Optimization Methods:
  • Vogel's Approximation Method: Balances cost minimization with good initial solutions
  • Least Cost Method: Prioritizes the lowest cost allocations first
  • Modified Distribution (MODI): Improves an initial solution through iterative optimization
